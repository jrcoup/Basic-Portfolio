About Me

My name is Jonathan Coup and I am an eletrical engineer at Burns and Mcdonnell. I graduated from KU in 2014 and am now pursuing my MBA at the KU edwards campus where im expected to graduate spring of 2018.

June 13th 2018 my wife Sarah and I brought a new baby boy into the world. His name is Liam and loves to spend time with mom and dad, even through the late hours of the night!

One of my favorite hobbies is cooking, where last winter I started smoking meat. They now call me Brisket Jonny around the office as my smoked meat is pretty darn good. I hope to start competing soon as I know my stuff stacks pretty high. I also started my own garden this year, planting mostly from seeds. I've got a lot of tomatoes in the back yard along with onions, strawberries, peppers, and herbs. Can't wait to start digging in to home grown produce. I also bake a lot of different breads, I find they go well with smoked meat. My favorite combination so far is pretzel buns with brisket and beer cheese. I'm getting hungry just thinking about it.
